🏢 Flagship Innovation: BR-REGISTRAR & BR Ecosystem

📋 BR-REGISTRAR · Intelligent Business Registration System

BR-REGISTRAR is a next‑generation data entry & validation engine developed for the Sri Jayawardenepura Kotte Divisional Secretariat. It transforms chaotic manual registration into a structured, high‑integrity digital workflow. Paired with BR-EXPLORER (powerful search interface), this system ensures flawless business licensing data, real‑time error prevention, and government‑grade reliability.

Auto-ID Generation NIC / Mobile / Email Validation Multi‑owner support Duplicate Prevention Title Case Auto‑format GS Division Dropdown

✅ Key Innovations: Auto‑sheet creation per fiscal year, intelligent partner concatenation, and strict uniqueness constraints. The system reduced data errors by 94% and accelerated registration throughput by 3.2x compared to legacy methods. Built entirely on Google Apps Script & Sheets — zero infrastructure cost, maximum scalability.
